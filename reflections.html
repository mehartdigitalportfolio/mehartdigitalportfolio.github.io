Mid-year Reflection Questions and Responses:

1) My friend group is pretty broad. I get along with everyone and if someone wants to join in on an activity, everyone is pretty welcoming. I have many friends in different grades from electives, clubs, and extra-cirriculars. I always encourage involving people from different grade levels in activities. 2) Outside of school, I do a lot of charity fundraising. For example, I have raised money for the Susan G Comen foundation and have worked with the Leukemia Lymphoma Society student branch to spread the word. I also participate in sports such as tennis and basketball. 3) I believe I am consistent in completing my work on time. I use a calendar to help me divide out and plan my homework.
